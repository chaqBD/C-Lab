Research Division

Original Research &
Empirical Frameworks

C-Lab produces peer-reviewed and policy-grade research that bridges narrative economics, AI methodology, and applied macroeconomics for Africa.

News-Based Risk Measurement
AERI/ARS framework development, cross-country heterogeneity analysis, and narrative economics for African economies.
Economic Policy Uncertainty
54-country African EPU indices using Baker et al. (2016) methodology, with AfDB mandate and multilingual keyword support.
Inflation Dynamics & Forecasting
Monthly Nigeria CPI forecasting, African inflation nowcasting, exchange-rate pass-through, and price expectation modelling.
Macro Surveillance & Early Warning
Debt sustainability, fiscal space analysis, sovereign-bank nexus, and real-time balance-of-payments stress indicators.
Climate–Economy Nexus
Disaster-linked fiscal stress, commodity cycle integration, and climate risk channels in African macroeconomic indicators.
Development & Institutional Economics
FX dynamics, commodity dependence, political economy of adjustment, and structural transformation in African economies.
